Instruction

  • Choose City1.
  • Choose City2 (optional).
  • Choose a graph type. Default is a bar graph and average temperature.
  • Imapct of Temp/Precip to the climate similarity can be changed using the slider. Default value is 70%.
  • Press Submit.
  • When city, graph type etc is changed, press Sumbit again.
    • Bar graph or hythergarph of City1, City2 will be displayed.
    • Table lists top 5 cities with climate similar to City1 by region. The numbers are climate similarity indices.

Data

  • Japanese climate data were retrieved from here: https://weather.time-j.net .
  • climate data of other regions were retrieved from here https://www.weatherbase.com .
  • Wether similarity index is calculated as sum of square difference of 2 cities temperature/precipitation of each month.
  • Wether similarity index value close to 0 indicates similar climate. The index values vary between 0 and 28.
  • Region All is any region excluding the region of City1.
  • Japan is considered a separate region, not included in Asia.

Calculation of Climate Similarity Index

  • Summer and winter months are weighted higher than other months (by a factor of 1.5 to 3.0).
  • Impact of temperature/precipitation to the climate simiarlity index is 70:30 at default. When either temperature or precipitation data is missing, only avaialbe data are used.
  • Data of southern hemisphere are shifted by 6 months to match summer/winter.
  • This caluclation method is not based on prior publication.
